Review process

Keep the directory useful, current, and ready for presenters.

Profiles should give audiences and organizers enough context to understand the artist, evaluate fit, and make contact.

Who can be featured

Current institute artists, alumni, invited guests, external collaborators, and nominated musicians with a clear connection to the network.

What to include

Short biography, discipline, base city, repertoire or project focus, media links, booking availability, and a reliable contact route.

How profiles stay current

Review event dates, media links, availability, and contact details each season so presenters do not reach stale information.

Permissions and consent

Only submit details, media links, and booking contacts that the musician has approved for public promotion.

Open call

Nominate a musician or submit your own profile.

Collect biographies, instruments or voice type, repertoire interests, media links, location, institute relationship, and booking contact details.
